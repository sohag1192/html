About Sarker Net

Sarker Net is a Internet Service Provider having license from Bangladesh Telecommunications Regulatory Commission (BTRC).
We offer broadband internet and private data communication solutions over fiber optic, wireless and satellite technology through out the country. Sarker Net with its state-of-the-art redundant backbone infrastructure assures 100% uptime while providing ultimate clients satisfaction no matter how small a client may be. The Network Operation Center (NOC) is staffed 24/7 basis to respond and fix any issues related to connectivity.

Our Mission

To provide complete, quality ICT solutions to our clients by incorporating sensible and cost-effective technologies. Multiple network platforms are being supported by a team of customer-oriented quality resource people whose overriding priority is to provide professional service to our valued customers resulting in complete customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To connect the mass people of Bangladesh in the ever growing Information Highway by empowering them with digital information.
